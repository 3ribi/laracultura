Board Members

Youssef CHGHAICH

President/Founder

Moroccan film and theater actor, musician, and training facilitator. He is the president/founder of LaraCultura NGO and a freelance project coordinator with experience working on Erasmus plus and European solidarity corps projects for over two years. He has also been involved in coordinating international festivals, workshops, training courses, conferences, and artistic activities in Morocco and abroad. He runs several projects for children and young people in Larache and underprivileged neighborhoods and has developed new methods for engaging disadvantaged populations.



Jihad Nehal

Vice president

An active citizen, with more than 15 years of experience in the local and international youth work field, his role in the NGO is diversified into project management and legal consulting based on his educational background majoring in international law. He has strong network being a member of the Central Committee and National Coordinator of the Foreign Relations Committee of the Democratic Youth Movement. Jihad is also a significant and major contributor to the output of a social study book dedicated to children with difficult circumstances as part of his social work experiences.



Imane ASBAI

Treasurer

Junior entrepreneurial consultant who has completed internships in the field of entrepreneurship. She has worked with young people in urban and rural areas to raise their awareness and support their integration into the world of entrepreneurship. She has a strong network in the entrepreneurial field and also works as a coordinator for a Swedish NGO, organizing and implementing projects funded by the Swedish Institute in Morocco that focus on culture and young people with fewer opportunities.



Amine El Aribi

Vise treasurer

A dedicated student of informatic development currently. He has a natural talent for programming languages and is skilled in building websites. In addition to his studies, Amine is also deeply passionate about art and music and is always looking to learn more about different cultures and their artistic and musical traditions. He is a curious and lifelong learner who is eager to explore new subjects and acquire new knowledge. Amine is a hardworking individual who is committed to continuously improving his skills and staying current with developments in his field.



Dahab Serroukh

Secretary

She is a BA degree graduate in English literature, she has a strong skills in community management and social media. As a project manager assistant, she has gained valuable experience through her contribution in managing our projects. Dahab is eager to contribute to the leadership of future international projects, particularly those that focus on providing opportunities for young people with fewer opportunities.
