Rasism in Morocco

episode 6:

The sixth episode of Achbanlek is here 🤩! The topic of the episode has been chosen based on your votes, and this time we'll be discussing the topic of racism in Morocco! Racism is the beliefs or behaviors that a certain groups of people have allowing them to feel superior from other people. And this is generally based on previous prejudice, discrimination, or antagonism directed against other category of people due to their race, skin color, culture, believes or ethnicity. We can notice these behaviors even in our country! Between mountain people and Rifi’s, between south and north and sometimes even against South African immigrants.
