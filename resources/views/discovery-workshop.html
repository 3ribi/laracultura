Discovery Workshop

trimestery
  • Posted By : Amine EL ARIBI
  • |
  • Time : once every trimester

Welcome to an expansive initiative, a multifaceted project meticulously designed to empower and enrich the lives of young individuals as they navigate the intricacies of everyday existence. This comprehensive endeavor comprises a series of monthly workshops, each meticulously crafted to utilize non-formal learning tools and foster the holistic development of local youth, offering them fresh perspectives and avenues for personal and professional growth.

Diving into diverse realms, our workshops serve as dynamic platforms for skill-building, covering an array of topics that span the spectrum of contemporary life. From the intricacies of graphic design to the adoption of eco-friendly life hacks, the exploration of entrepreneurial pursuits, and the cultivation of artistic expression through arts and crafts, these sessions cater to a broad spectrum of interests and aspirations.

At the heart of our project lies a commitment to not only enhancing individual capabilities but also opening doors to new opportunities. Through interactive and engaging methodologies, participants are not merely passive learners but active contributors to their own learning journey. The project's architecture is rooted in the principles of non-formal education, where experiential learning takes precedence, creating an environment conducive to creativity, critical thinking, and practical application.

An inherent focus on Erasmus+ and European Solidarity Corps (ESC) programs adds a global dimension to our efforts, encouraging participants to think beyond local boundaries and explore international avenues. By delving into these programs, participants gain insights into diverse cultures, collaborative initiatives, and the broader landscape of opportunities available to them.

Embark on this transformative journey with us, where each workshop is not just a lesson but a stepping stone towards a more empowered, capable, and well-rounded future. Join us as we collectively shape a generation equipped with the skills and mindset needed to thrive in the complexities of today's world. This project is more than a series of workshops; it is a catalyst for personal and community-wide transformation.

Erasmus+ and ESC programs

episode 1:

The first episode will be about Discovering Erasmus plus and European Solidarity Corps programs of the European Union. Together, we will find out what these programs are, and we will also discover how to become involved in them.

Graphic Design Basics: Adobe Illustrator

episode 2:

This month's theme will be about Graphic Design Basics Using Adobe Illustrator. Come hang out with us to discover how we can make use of this powerful tool to make jaw-dropping graphic works of art.

Eco-Friendly Lifehacks

episode 3:

We had a special guest Abdelhamid Zoubir that did host us into his vast beautiful garden, an environmental laboratory of sorts. Along with Dr.Oumaima Harkousse, a PhD graduate in Plants Biotechnology and an environmental activist, we discovered how we can live in harmony with the environment, and exploring lifehacks that we can use in our day to day life to live a more eco-friendly lifestyle.
